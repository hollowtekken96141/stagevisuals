TEKKEN96141 INTERACTIVE STAGE VISUALS

Instructions

Press F11 to enter full screen mode. Use scroll wheel or scroll to adjust the speed of the visualizer:

  • Scroll down to increase speed (for high-intensity moments)
  • At high speeds, scroll back and forth to "ping-pong" the text
  • Click the screen to change the image set
  • Look in the top right corner to see the current time interval in milliseconds.
